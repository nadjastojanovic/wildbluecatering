Takeout Food Services

We are suspending our Catering operations and instead running an assisted living facility. We want to deliver our well-known flavor and impeccable recipes to everyone staying at home right now.

Emergency Culinary Staff

Due to COVID-19, we were a Kitchen Relief Team for an assisted living facility of 265 residents that had staff go down and the balance of the staff had to be quarantined.

In response to COVID-19, we are no longer operating as a catering service. Instead, we are running a facility and delivering food right to your address while you are staying at home. Order takeout now and have delicious food waiting at your doorstep in less than an hour. Choose from our selection of high-quality meals!
